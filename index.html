Pet Adoption App & Website Design



PetStar is an app and website that uses astrology to help people find their perfect pet. Users are prompted to take the star sign compatibility quiz which pairs them with compatible animals from nearby shelters. The user can also browse available pets by zodiac signs, save favorites, and request scheduled visits. PetStar was created with the intention of promoting pet adoption. This project was created using Adobe XD.

Currency Redesign



A redesign of US currency dimensions 2.61" x 6.14", which pay tribute to three influential and historic Native American women. The $100 bill features Elizabeth Wanamaker Peratrovich who was an American civil rights activist, Grand President of the Alaska Native Sisterhood, and member of the Tlingit nation who worked for equality on behalf of Alaska Natives. The $20 bill features Lyda Conley, who was a Wyandot-American lawyer of Native American and European descent, the first woman admitted to the Kansas Bar Association. She was notable for her campaign to prevent the sale and development of the Huron Cemetery in Kansas City, now known as the Wyandot National Burying Ground. The $5 bill features Susan La Flesche Picotte who was a doctor and reformer in the late 19th century. She is widely acknowledged as one of the first Indigenous peoples, and the first Indigenous woman, to earn a medical degree. All bills feature different nation logos pertaining to each woman's nation.

Visual Identity Branding



A visual identity and logograph that represents the small business Secretly Yours. Secretly Yours is a mastectomy boutique located in Palm Beach Gardens, Florida. Founded in 2002 the boutique provides services and products services to women who have undergone breast surgery due to breast cancer. The identity standards of the business are centered around breast cancer awareness and support. This worked as the foundation for my identity system, which consists of a variety of pinks, breast cancer ribbons, and a woman with the breast cancer ribbon placed where her missing breast is.
